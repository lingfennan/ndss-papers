Program Chairs' Message


It is a great pleasure to usher in the first NDSS of the new millennium! As a direct result of the worldwide "Internet-ization," the last decade has seen an unprecedented growth in the importance of computer and network security as well as increased awareness of various risks, threats and attacks. Consequently, our collective mission becomes much harder as the relevant technical advances in the research community are being utilized faster and scrutinized more than ever before.

A total of 51 submissions were received this year. The average submission quality was very high despite the increasing number of competing venues. Submissions came from around the world, attesting to the international flavor of NDSS.

The program committee met on Friday the 13th (of August). Despite the ominous date, the meeting was an overwhelming success resulting in the selection of 15 outstanding papers on a broad range of subjects. They represent the state-of-the-art in network and distributed systems security and demonstrate the talents and dedication of the authors.

Many individuals are responsible for the success of NDSS 2000. First and foremost, we are grateful to the authors of all submitted papers for their patronage and for the hard work invested in submissions. The authors are both the backbone and the target audience of this conference.

Members of the program committee have done a wonderful job and we cannot thank them enough for their time and effort in reviewing papers, participating in the PC meeting and assisting in many related activities. It has been a pleasure and an honor to work with them. We are equally grateful to the delegated reviewers for their selfless community service.

A special word of thanks to Steve Welke who, as General Chair, shared his knowledge and experience on numerous occasions. We are grateful to the Publicity Chair, Dave Balenson, who has done -- once again -- a great job promoting and advertising NDSS. We also thank Doug Maughan, the Tutorials Chair, for putting together an outstanding selection of tutorials on timely and important topics.



Gene Tsudik

I am very proud of my affiliation with NDSS and I sincerely appreciate the opportunity to serve as a PC member and a Program Chair.
